Our Services

At Xi'an Pengjun Trading Co., Ltd., we are committed to delivering reliable, high-quality, and customized solutions across a wide range of industries. Our core operations span three major sectors: Marine Services, Medical Supplies, and Global Trading. Below is a comprehensive overview of our specialized services

1.Marine Sector Services – Bangladesh Operations

Our marine division provides end-to-end support for vessels operating in and around Bangladeshi ports. We are proud to be a trusted partner for shipping companies, offering:

  • Ship Repair & Maintenance:

    • Mechanical, electrical, and structural repairs for all types of vessels. We ensure quick turnaround times with expert technical crews and port-side services.

  • Marine Supply & Provisions:

    • Supply of fresh and dry provisions, bonded stores, deck and engine stores, safety gear, and cleaning materials directly to vessels at berth or anchorage.

  • Spare Parts & Equipment Delivery:

    • Sourcing and delivering genuine OEM and aftermarket spare parts, marine engines, pumps, compressors, valves, and other onboard equipment.

  • Port Agency & Technical Support:

    • Handling vessel clearance, documentation, coordination with local authorities, and offering 24/7 technical support during port calls.

  • Emergency Services:

    • On-call support for urgent repairs, equipment failure, or provisioning needs, ensuring vessels remain operational with minimal downtime.

2.Medical Supplies & Equipment

We provide reliable and compliant medical products and solutions to healthcare institutions, clinics, and distributors worldwide. Our offerings include:

  • Laboratory Supplies:

    • Blood sample test tubes, centrifuge tubes, syringes, and other disposable lab consumables used in diagnostics and testing.

  • Medical Consumables:

    • Needles, gloves, masks, IV sets, wound dressings, and sterilization pouches available in bulk packaging for institutional supply.

  • Mobility & Rehabilitation Products:

    • Wheelchairs (manual and electric), walkers, crutches, and hospital beds designed for patient mobility, comfort, and care.

  • Diagnostic & Hospital Equipment:

    • Sourcing and exporting ECG machines, ultrasound systems, thermometers, patient monitors, and custom-requested devices to meet healthcare needs.

  • Procurement & Logistics:

    • Global sourcing, quality assurance, regulatory compliance, and efficient shipping for medical supplies, including urgent bulk orders.

3.Global Trading – Multisector Commodities

Our marine division provides end-to-end support for vessels operating in and around Bangladeshi ports. We are proud to be a trusted partner for shipping companies, offering:

  • Palm Oil and Edible Oils:

    • Monthly supply and export of refined palm oil in bulk or packaged form. Sourcing from certified producers for delivery to ports such as Jeddah Islamic Port, Saudi Arabia.

  • Food & Agro Products:

    • Trading of rice, sugar, canned foods, beverages, and other packaged FMCG products for retail and wholesale distribution.

  • Electrical & Electronic Goods:

    • Industrial electronics, home appliances, solar products, and electrical components sourced from trusted manufacturers for international distribution.

  • Machinery & Equipment:

    • Construction, agricultural, and industrial machinery including engines, generators, pumps, and production-line equipment for a wide range of sectors.

  • Chemicals & Raw Materials:

    • Supply of industrial chemicals, solvents, and raw materials used in manufacturing, cleaning, or processing industries. Available with MSDS documentation and global shipping.

4.Eco-Friendly Consumer Products

Our marine division provides end-to-end support for vessels operating in and around Bangladeshi ports. We are proud to be a trusted partner for shipping companies, offering

  • Disposable Tableware & Packaging

    • Biodegradable plates, bowls, cups, straws, cutlery, and food containers made from plant-based materials such as bagasse, cornstarch, and bamboo.

  • Compostable Food Packaging

    • Eco-friendly packaging solutions for food service, catering, restaurants, and retail—suitable for hot and cold items.

  • Recyclable Household Products

    • Paper napkins, tissue rolls, eco bags, and reusable cleaning supplies for household and commercial use.

  • Sustainable Retail & Wholesale Supply

    • Bulk supply of certified eco-products for supermarkets, online retailers, and environmentally-conscious brands.
